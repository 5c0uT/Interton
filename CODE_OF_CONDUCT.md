# Code of Conduct

## EN

### Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

### Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and considerate in language and tone.
- Being constructive with feedback and reviews.
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing when appropriate.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Trolling, personal attacks, or sustained disruption of discussions.
- Publishing others' private information without permission (doxxing).
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

### Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

### Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

- Prefer a private report to the repository owner/maintainers via GitHub profile contact (the safest available channel in this project setup).
- If you cannot use a private channel, open an issue and clearly mark it as a Code of Conduct report.
- For immediate threats or safety concerns, contact local emergency services.

When reporting, include:

- What happened and where (links if possible).
- When it happened.
- Who was involved.
- Any relevant screenshots/logs (redact sensitive data).

### Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will follow these community impact guidelines:

1. Correction: A private, written warning with clarification.
2. Warning: A formal warning with consequences for continued behavior.
3. Temporary ban: Temporary restriction from interactions/contributions.
4. Permanent ban: Permanent removal from the community/project spaces.

### Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.

## RU

### Обязательство

Мы стремимся сделать участие в проекте безопасным и уважительным для всех, вне зависимости от личных характеристик, опыта, происхождения, идентичности и взглядов.

### Нормы

Приемлемо:

- Уважительное общение, конструктивный фидбек, доброжелательный тон.
- Фокус на улучшении проекта, а не на личности.
- Признание ошибок и готовность исправляться.

Неприемлемо:

- Оскорбления, травля, домогательства, дискриминация.
- Личные атаки, троллинг, умышленный срыв обсуждений.
- Публикация приватной информации без разрешения.

### Как сообщить

Если вы столкнулись с нарушением:

- По возможности используйте приватный канал связи с владельцем/мейнтейнерами через GitHub профиль.
- Если приватно нельзя, создайте issue и явно отметьте, что это сообщение по Code of Conduct.
- При угрозе безопасности обращайтесь в экстренные службы.

Опишите: что произошло, где (с ссылками), когда, кто участвовал, приложите материалы (с удалением чувствительных данных).
